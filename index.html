Tech and Digital Life: Digital Detox

In today's hyper-connected world, constant notifications and endless scrolling can leave us feeling overwhelmed, distracted, and drained. A digital detox is the intentional act of stepping away from screens to reconnect with what truly matters - your time, your focus, and your well-being.

Even small changes like turning off notifications, setting screen-free hours, or spending time outdoors can make a big difference. These simple habits help improve mental clarity, boost productivity, and strengthen your relationships.

Start your journey toward a more balanced, mindful life today.
