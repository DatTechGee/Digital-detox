5 Simple Ways to Start Your Digital Detox Today

Start small by muting non-essential notifications and setting two screen-free windows each day. These tiny changes lower stress and improve attention quickly.

Try moving social apps off your home screen, keeping your phone out of reach while working, and using a simple evening routine without screens. Consistency matters more than perfection.

Review your progress weekly and celebrate wins. A digital detox is not about quitting technology, it is about choosing when and how you use it.
